Binding - refers to the process of converting identifiers (such as
          variable and performance names) into addresses.

Its types - 

1.Static Binding - (Early binding)
    e.g. function calls, overloaded function calls, 
         and overloaded operators are done at compile-time.

2.Dynamic Binding - (Late binding)
    e.g. Function pointers, Virtual functions are examples of late binding
